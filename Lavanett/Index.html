About us

Over two decades ago, a love story began to unfold. Gabriel Casas, the visionary behind Lavanett, found in Vanessa the love of his life. With time, Gabriel and Vanessa (affectionately called "La Vane") nurtured a precious secret deep within their hearts: Lavanett, led by a closely-knit family from a charming neighborhood in Buenos Aires.

In 2008, Gabriel forged a fruitful business relationship with Alex, a provider of top-quality products. After 8 years together, Gabriel and "La Vane" gave birth to Lavanett: a brand synonymous with excellence, style, design, elegance, and modernity. Its impact resonates in Argentina, Chile, Peru, and the recent triumph in Mexico. Today, they embark on a new chapter: conquering the United States. With a legacy of over 16 years, Lavanett Corp is ready for the journey ahead, with Canada and Europe as our next destinations.
